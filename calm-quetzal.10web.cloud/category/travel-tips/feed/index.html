Here are some essential travel tips to ensure a smooth and enjoyable journey. First, consider the best time to visit, taking into account the weather and local festivals. Pack light clothing suitable for the tropical climate. Don’t forget sunscreen, insect repellent, and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.

Transportation within Perlis is relatively easy. You can rent a car, hire a taxi, or use ride-hailing services. Public transportation is also available, but it may be less frequent. Be sure to familiarize yourself with the local customs and etiquette. Respect the local culture and traditions.

Accommodation options in Perlis range from budget-friendly guesthouses to luxurious resorts. Book your accommodation in advance, especially during peak season. Learn a few basic Malay phrases to communicate with the locals. With these travel tips, you’re well-prepared to explore the beauty and charm of Perlis.
